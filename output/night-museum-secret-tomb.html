Night at the Museum: Secret of the Tomb

Posted on Fri 19 December 2014 in Projects

A lot of hard work and crazy hours went in to creating the visual effect for Night at the Museum: Secret of the Tomb. It's no surprise the film made the short-list for the 2014 Oscar for Visual Effects. I was lucky enough to spend a short amount of time on the show, and I know first hand the amount of effort that went into every shot. Check out the trailer below (and watch for the costume I helped create around 1:09).
