Transformers: The Last Knight

Posted on Sat 05 August 2017 in Projects

I had the privilege of joining the ILM Vancouver creature department just in time to help finish Transformers: The Last Knight. I worked on in-shot simulations for the bits and bobs that hang off the robots. Most of my work was in the junkyard sequence and a couple of shots towards the beginning of the film. And I got to help on Optimus Prime for a shot in the trailer below (around the 1:48 mark).
