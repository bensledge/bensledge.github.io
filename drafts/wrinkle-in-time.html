A Wrinkle in Time

Posted on Sat 10 March 2018 in Projects

Based on Madeleine L'Engle's beloved children's book, A Wrinkle in Time follows Meg, Calvin and Charles Wallace through time and space to try and save Meg's father. Industrial Light and Magic was tasked with fulfilling director Ava DuVernay's vision for the planet Uriel. On the creature team, we were responsible for creating Mrs. Whatsit in her flying form; and digital doubles of the kids. Personally, I worked on the animation/deformation rig for the flying Mrs. Whatsit, the cloth rig for Meg, and simulation shot work through our sequence.
