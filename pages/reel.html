ROBOREPAIR **1 | Character Rigging: "ROBOREPAIR" Short Film** Designed a fully articulated character rig in Maya. The rig features the ability to switch between three arm attachments and FK/IK switching on the arms and legs. When the arms and legs are in IK mode, the ball joints rotate to keep the limb in the socket of the ball joints. The rig features a torso rocker with an arbitrary pivot around the waist.

FINGERMOTION **6 | Automatic Finger Animation Research** Implemented a system for automatically generating handover animation from wrist movement. Created a database of finger motions from motion capture data. Input wrist motion was segmented into motion clips and matched with finger motions from the database.
